Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you plan your Life Luxury experience with confidence.

What’s included in a yacht booking?

Yacht experiences typically include a private vessel, professional crew, onboard amenities, and itinerary planning. Add-ons like land to yacht or helicopter access, gourmet catering, chef service, champagne packages, water toys, and special celebrations can be arranged based on your package and location.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking as early as possible—especially for weekends, holidays, and peak travel seasons. For signature experiences, 2–4 weeks is ideal. Last-minute requests are sometimes possible depending on availability.

Deposits & cancellations?

Most bookings require a deposit to reserve your date. Cancellation and rescheduling policies vary by operator, season, and package. We’ll provide clear terms before you confirm, so you know exactly what’s refundable and what’s not.

What locations do you serve?

Life Luxury coordinates experiences in select destinations and can arrange travel-based itineraries upon request. Tell us your preferred location and dates, and we’ll confirm availability and options.

Can I bundle helicopter + limo + jet?

Yes. Many clients choose bundled itineraries (example: private jet arrival → chauffeured transfer → helicopter experience → yacht departure). Our concierge team designs the schedule so everything flows seamlessly.

Are crews included?

Yes—yachts include a professional crew. Flight crews and chauffeurs are included for aircraft and limousine services through our licensed partners. Specific crew details depend on the selected operator and package.

Dress code / special requests?

Dress is typically “resort elegant” for dinners and “smart casual” for daytime experiences. We can arrange special requests like flowers, photographers, proposals, dietary needs, custom music, and surprise reveals—just tell us what you want.

Weather backup plans?

Safety comes first. If weather impacts your experience, options may include adjusted timing, alternate routes, rescheduling, or operator policies based on conditions. We’ll guide you through the best option for a smooth experience.
