Yacht FAQ

Quick answers to help you book with confidence.

How does pricing work?

Membership gives you priority access and concierge planning. Your final quote depends on destination, dates, yacht size, and inclusions.

What’s included in most yacht experiences?

Typically: captain + crew, concierge planning, accommodations, and comfort amenities. Chef, beverages, excursions, and toys can be added based on your preferences.

How far in advance should I book?

Peak dates book quickly. If you can, plan 4–8 weeks out. For special occasions, earlier is even better.

Is this beginner-friendly?

Absolutely. You don’t need yacht experience—your crew and concierge guide you through the entire journey.

What happens if weather changes?

Safety comes first. We’ll coordinate alternatives such as rescheduling, route adjustments, or destination swaps depending on conditions and policies.
